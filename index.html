About the Overwatch League

Overwatch League is the competitive e-sports scene of the popular, award-winning game Overwatch. Teams made up of 6 players who specialize in each type of game role (DPS,Flex,Tank,Support), compete against each other each week towards the stage winner prize of $100,000 and the grand prize of $1,000,000. Matches take place each week in an e-sports arena in the Los Angeles area.

The Overwatch league is split up into 4 stages; each stage contains 5 weeks of play, with 3 matches a day (Wednesday-Saturday), culminating in a playoff match at the end of the fifth week to determine the winner of each stage. At the end of Stage 4 a grand champion will be chosen determined by the previous winnings and the outcome of the final game.

About New York Excelsior

New York Excelsior is the current top team of the Overwatch League. Most of the players have been in the competitive Overwatch scene since its beginning in 2016, and have competed in both Apex and the Overwatch World Cup. Saebyeolbe and Mano have been a part of Team South Korea who competed for, and won, the Overwatch World cup in 2016 and 2017. New York Excelsior were the runners up of stage 1 and the winners of stage 2. They continue into the 3rd stage in first place overall, and the hopeful winners of the League championship.
